Ordering
Visit wingstop.com or download the Wingstop app, select your nearest location, build your order from our full menu, and check out. You can order for pickup or delivery through our partners.
Wingstop partners with DoorDash, Uber Eats, Grubhub, and Postmates depending on your region. Delivery availability varies by location. Order directly through our app or website for the best experience and to earn Rewards points.

Menu & Flavors
Wingstop offers 11 signature flavors: Lemon Pepper, Original Hot, Atomic, Cajun, Garlic Parmesan, Hawaiian, Hickory Smoked BBQ, Korean Q, Mango Habanero, Louisiana Rub, and Mild. Limited edition flavors are introduced seasonally via our Flavor Lab program.
Bone-in wings are the classic whole wings — drumette and flat — known for their juicy, rich meat. Boneless wings are whole chunks of white breast meat, breaded and sauced. Both can be ordered in any of our 11 flavors.
Yes. Our menu includes Chicken Tenders, seasoned Fries (in multiple flavors), Cheese Sauce, Veggie Sticks, soft drinks, and bottled water. We regularly introduce limited-time sides and dipping sauces.
Atomic is our hottest flavor — it's a five-pepper blend designed for serious heat seekers. A close second is Mango Habanero, which brings both fruity sweetness and significant heat. Mild and Garlic Parmesan are on the opposite end with no heat at all.
Nutrition
Full nutritional details are available on our website's Nutrition page and within the Wingstop app when ordering. Calorie counts are shown directly on the menu at all locations.
Bone-in wings in our dry rub flavors (Louisiana Rub, Lemon Pepper) are gluten-free. However, our kitchens are not certified gluten-free environments. If you have a severe allergy, please consult our nutrition guide and speak with your local location before ordering.
Currently, Wingstop's menu is centered on chicken. We offer seasoned fries and veggie sticks as sides. We're always exploring menu innovation and appreciate the feedback.
Rewards
Wingstop Rewards is our free loyalty program. Earn 1 point per $1 spent. Redeem points for free wings and menu items. Members also get early access to new flavors, exclusive event invites, and birthday perks.
Sign up for free at wingstop.com/rewards or in the Wingstop app. Create an account with your email, and you'll start earning points immediately on your first order.
Points expire after 6 months of account inactivity. Any qualifying purchase resets the inactivity clock. Check your account balance anytime in the app or on our website.
Franchising
Start by submitting a franchise inquiry through our website. You'll connect with our development team to discuss market availability, financial requirements, and the application process. We look for passionate operators with strong business backgrounds.
The total investment for a new Wingstop varies by market and location. Initial franchise fees, build-out costs, and working capital requirements are detailed in our Franchise Disclosure Document, which is shared during the qualification process.
International
Wingstop operates in 12+ countries including the United States, Mexico, Canada, Colombia, Panama, United Kingdom, France, Netherlands, UAE, Saudi Arabia, Bahrain, Singapore, Indonesia, and the Philippines.
Core flavors are consistent worldwide. Some markets also offer locally inspired limited editions. International operators work with our culinary team to introduce flavors that resonate locally while maintaining the Wingstop quality standard.
