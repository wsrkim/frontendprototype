Our History

The Journey

1994

Garland, Texas — The Beginning

Antonio Swad opens the first Wingstop in Garland, Texas. The concept is simple: dedicated to the wing, nothing else. The focus and the flavors immediately resonate with guests.

1997

First Franchise Opens

The franchise model launches, inviting passionate entrepreneurs to bring Wingstop's bold flavor to their communities. The response is immediate and overwhelming.

2003

200 Locations

A major milestone: 200 locations serving wings across the United States. The brand's focus on flavor — not gimmicks — earns a deeply loyal following.

2007

International Expansion Begins

Wingstop crosses its first border, entering the Mexican market. The bold flavors translate instantly, laying the groundwork for a global brand.

2012

IPO on NASDAQ: WING

Wingstop Inc. goes public on the NASDAQ exchange, cementing its status as one of the fastest-growing restaurant chains in America.

2018

1,000+ Locations

The network crosses 1,000 locations — a landmark that speaks to the brand's unique power: simple, focused, bold. Digital ordering begins reshaping the experience.

2021

Digital-First Transformation

Wingstop commits fully to a digital-first model — one of the first major QSR brands to do so. Digital orders account for 65%+ of total sales.

2026

The World's Flavor Authority

1,900+ locations. 12+ countries. A billion wings served. Wingstop enters its next chapter — more flavors, more markets, and an unwavering obsession with what made it great.

What We Believe

Our Pillars

01

Flavor First

Every menu decision starts with one question: is it bold enough? We reject mediocrity in every sauce, every seasoning, every plate.

02

Community Roots

We're franchise-powered, which means we're locally rooted. Our operators aren't just business owners — they're neighbors.

03

Relentless Craft

The wing is simple. Doing it right is not. We obsess over cook temperatures, marination time, and sauce balance every single day.

04

Bold by Design

We aren't a timid brand. We push flavors to the edge, celebrate those who love heat, and respect those who love sweet. All bold. All welcome.

05

Digital Native

We moved digital-first before it was a trend. Technology is part of how we deliver a better experience — from order to pick-up to rewards.

06

Global Soul

Flavor is a universal language. We build menus that respect local tastes while honoring the bold spirit that defines Wingstop everywhere.
